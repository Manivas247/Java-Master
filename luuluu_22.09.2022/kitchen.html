Straight Kitchen

Product Details


It is designed on available straight walls. Cooking and cleaning units comes in one line. This kitchen is essentially made for a smaller kitchen area. Such kitchens are designed by adding top and bottom cabinets for storage and all other elements to increase functionality and easy accessibilities. Our consultants will ensure convivence and optimization of your straight design and our huge range of exterior finish will bring absolute aesthetics to your kitchen.


An island shape is a freestanding piece of cabinetry made by using different modules that is placed in kitchen to supplement the countertop space. Add the island in any of your kitchen layout, provided the space permits. Our kitchen consultants place more focus on the development of intricate kitchen islands by adding sinks and cooking surfaces to them or by just making it a supplemental dining area


Typically , L-shape kitchen consists of two adjoining perpendicular walls which could be used for placing your kitchen cabinets. Ideally if space permits one side is used for sink and the other is used for cooking. In this layout, corner is an important place with maximum storage space, and we suggest that corner need to be deigned with accessories to ensure optimization of space.
