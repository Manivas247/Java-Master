Our Services

OCEAN FREIGHT TRANSPORTATION

The transportation logistics in today's economy plays a crucial role in preserving your customer relationships, sustaining the current working and creating new opportunities. At Bluee Lagoon Logistics, we take this mandate seriously by providing you with an efficient service. The key to success is our streamlined operations, experience, expertise and global reach. It is our endeavour to provide a highly flexible service in a timely and cost-effective manner in partnership with leading carriers. These relationships guarantee us the edge in space capacity and enable us to meet the growing sea freight requirements.

OCEAN FREIGHT EXCELLENCE

  • Reliable ocean-bound service for Full Container Loads (FCL)
  • Direct and Multi country Consolidation to optimize your LCL Import and Export
  • Expertise in handling Temperature controlled and Perishables cargo
  • Specialists in handling project cargo- ODC and Heavy Lift
  • RO, Break Bulk, Part and Full Charter services
  • Combined Air and Ocean freight services
  • Competitive pricing

CUSTOM CLEARANCE

Bluee Lagoon Logistics is fully equipped to serve as the first responder at critical customs clearance situations and undertake the responsibility in accordance with your needs and expectations. We Handle: Sea Imports and exports both LCL and FCL movements and Break Bulks and We offer:
  • Prompt turn-around for basic, integrated as well as value-added customs clearance services
  • Trained workforce which possesses an excellent understanding of trade requirements, procedures and customs tariff regulations
  • Services which ensure that you remain compliant at all times and avoid unnecessary delays and penalties
  • Professional preparation and error-free submission of all the necessary documents related to customs clearance activities.
  • Online tracking of pending shipments in real time
  • Specialists in handling Rice,automobile, electronics, machinery, pharma to name a few and range of other product related clearance
