About  Us

WORLD-CLASS OEM PARTNERS


In a globalized world, it is important to choose the right supply partners. Our unrivaled supply chain capabilities give us the ability to design products in response to latest global trends and bring them to India way ahead of our competition.


We source the raw materials, internal components & finished products of our expansive product range from 60+ World-Class OEM companies in Italy, France, Hungary, Turkey, China & Indonesia. Each manufacturer is selected only after meticulous factory and product quality inspections.

QUALITY IS IN OUR DNA


LuuLuu Homes has been built upon trust and partnerships with its client base. Quality and service have always been kept at the heart of the company’s value proposition.


LuuLuu Homes employs a large Quality Control (QC) team at manufacturing locations. Products must conform to strict quality standards. Every piece of luuluu Sanitary ware is inspected for pin-holes, micro-cracks & color consistency. Each Shower & Bathroom Fitting is tested under 1.6MPa water pressure to guarantee no leakage.

EXCELLENCE IN DESIGN


By working with some of India’s leading designers, we understand the latest trends. Our design-sense is exemplified by the consistent launching of globally trending Sanitaryware, Faucets & Showering Systemsn.


Our collection is comprised of contemporary products that exude luxury and combine exquisite style with state-of-the-art technology, unparalleled performance and the highest quality.

LUXURY MADE AFFORDABLE


We supply directly to limited showrooms across India without employing distributors; cutting out middle-men leads to substantial cost-savings which are passed to our customers. Cost-savings are also gained through our efficient adoption of global lean management techniques, latest warehouse management systems and advanced supply-chain capabilities.


This results in happy customers with world-class bathroom products purchased at best-in-class economical price-tags!
